About Hotel

Hotel New Tamanna Palace, Bhopal me guests ko comfortable aur pleasant stay ka experience milta hai. Yahaan AC aur Non-AC dono type ke rooms available hain, jo spacious, neat & clean interiors ke saath poori tarah se well‑maintained hain. Staff hamesha polite aur helpful rehta hai,jisse har guest ko ghar jaisa feel milta hai. Convenient location aur peaceful environment ke saath, ye hotel business aur leisure dono purpose ke travellers ke liye ek perfect choice hai.

  • Super Deluxe, Deluxe aur Executive Non‑AC rooms
  • 24x7 front desk / reception
  • Free Wi‑Fi
  • Parking facility
  • Room service
  • CCTV security
  • Banquet & Restaurant (as per availability)

Location: 3A, Sawan Nagar, Lalghati, Indore Road, Bhopal (M.P.)

Nearby: Lalghati Chowk, Airport Road, local market & transport easily available.
